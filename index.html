About Me

I am a transplant to the Bay Area, having moved here from the Midewest in mid-2017 for my wife's work. For the past 10 years I have been a top performing REATLOR in Cincinnati. Prior to real estate I was successful in helping two companies - in the bank servicing and civil infrastructure industries, respectively - grow from small, local businesses into regional and national market leaders.

My wife and I are proud, adoptive parents in a transracial family. I value spending time with family and friends, travel, the outdoors, and doing charity work. I decided to make the career transition into web development and coding to broaden my skillsets and create new business opportunities.
